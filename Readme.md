***Project Overview***
This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive sales analytics solution using the US Superstore dataset.
The goal of this project is to analyze business performance, identify profit drivers, highlight underperforming areas, and provide actionable insights to support data-driven decision-making.

**This project demonstrates strong capabilities in:**

-Data cleaning & transformation

-Data modeling in Power BI

-KPI building

-Interactive dashboard design

-Business-focused storytelling

***Tools & Technologies Used***

-Power BI (Data Modeling, DAX, Visualization)

-Power Query

-DAX Measures

-Data Cleaning & Transformation

-Excel (dataset source)

***Dataset***

-Dataset: Superstore Sales Dataset

-Contains:

-Orders

-Products

-Customers

-Regions

-Sales & Profit metrics

-Discounts

-Shipping & categories

-Time period covers multiple years of store transactions

***Business Objectives***

This dashboard is designed to help business stakeholders answer key questions such as:

-How are Sales & Profit trending over time?

-Who are our top customers?

-Which products generate the highest profit?

-Which regions are performing best?

-Are discounts impacting profitability?

-What is happening across Sales, Profit, Quantity & Margin?

***Key KPIs***

The top KPI section highlights:

-Total Sales

-Total Profit

-Profit Margin

-Total Quantity

-Number of Orders

These metrics give an immediate overview of overall business performance.

***Dashboard Visuals & Insights***

1-Time Trends

-Monthly Sales Trend

-Monthly Profit Trend

Helps monitor growth, seasonal performance, and business health over time.

2-Regional Performance

-Sales performance broken down by Region

-Helps identify:

-Best-performing regions

-Underperforming territories

-Regional business opportunities

3-Customer & Product Analysis

-Top 10 Customers by Sales

-Top 10 Products by Profit

Helps answer:

-Who are our most valuable customers?

-Which products bring the most money?

-Where should marketing & upsell efforts focus?

***Final Key Insights (Compact Version)***

-Sales show consistent growth with noticeable seasonal fluctuations.

-Profit does not always follow Sales — some high-sales months have lower profitability.

-Certain regions outperform others, highlighting geographic business strengths.

-A small number of customers contribute significantly to overall revenue.

-Top products drive major profit, indicating key inventory priorities.
